ALT Corridor — Recreation Capacity Dashboard

Interactive analysis of where recreation assets create opportunity and where tourism might create pressure across 27 towns along the Allegheny Trail corridor in West Virginia. Part of the ALT-EXP economic impact study. Combines a recreation asset inventory (26 public lands, 4 private/commercial facilities), EPA water system data, WVGES karst geology, and business density into a per-town opportunity/pressure scorecard with quadrant visualization, sortable data table, and an interactive map.

Built with: Python (GeoPandas, Folium), HTML/CSS/JS, EPA SDWIS, WVGES 1968 karst layer, US Census TIGER
